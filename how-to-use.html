FAQ

Where do I find the CSV export in my bank app?
Every bank is different, but look for "Statements", "Download transactions", or "Export" in your account or card settings — usually under Transaction History. Most major banks support CSV export from their website (desktop works better than mobile for this).
My CSV doesn't work. What format does it need?
The dashboard expects 5 columns in this order: date, merchant name, debit amount, credit amount, balance. Most bank exports already follow this layout. If yours doesn't, open the CSV in Excel or Google Sheets and reorder the columns.
Can I upload multiple months at once?
Yes — just drop multiple CSV files at the same time. The dashboard merges and deduplicates everything automatically.
Does it work with any currency?
Yes. Amounts display exactly as they appear in your CSV — just make sure all your files use the same currency for accurate totals.
Will my data be saved for next time?
No — each session starts fresh. Just re-upload your CSV when you want to use it again. Think of it like a calculator: you bring the numbers, it does the work.
